project 2

Several reports have been reported about personalized guns or smart guns. Many say smart guns have the potential to save a lot of human lives and also the socio-economic and political as well as the psychological effects all associated with gun violence. There are two types of smart guns produced till now: biometrically based trigger locks and radio frequency identification trigger locks. Although their mechanism is different, both types are designed to be activated only by the registered user. The project here I am presenting is also a user authorized smart gun but what differentiates it from the rest is the addition of GPS to track the location of the machine and restrict the location it will only be able to work. By doing this we can diminish the substantial number of deaths registered daily from homicides, suicides, misusage of a firearm, and generally from gun violence.
